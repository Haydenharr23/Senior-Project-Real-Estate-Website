West Des Moines Market Snapshot

West Des Moines remains a draw for families and professionals thanks to strong schools, retail, and commute options to downtown Des Moines.

What buyers are looking for

Updated kitchens, open layouts, and good school districts continue to rank high. Homes that are move-in ready often receive more attention than projects priced similarly.

For sellers

Pricing and presentation still drive results. Comparable sales in your specific neighborhood matter more than county-wide averages—your agent should walk you through recent closed sales nearby.

For buyers

Be ready with financing and a clear list of must-haves versus nice-to-haves. In competitive pockets, flexibility on closing dates can help your offer stand out.

Market conditions change—reach out for a conversation about your goals and timing.
