Moving to Des Moines: A Short Checklist

Whether you are relocating for work or family, a little planning makes landing in the metro smoother.

Before you arrive

  • Research neighborhoods that fit your commute, schools, and lifestyle.
  • Line up temporary housing if you need time to tour homes in person.
  • Transfer or establish utilities, internet, and mail forwarding.

After you move

  • Update your driver’s license and vehicle registration per Iowa timelines.
  • Register to vote and find local healthcare providers.
  • Explore—farmers markets, trails, and downtown events help you feel at home quickly.

New to the area and house hunting? I would love to help you narrow down the right part of the metro.
