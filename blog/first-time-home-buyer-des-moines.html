First-Time Home Buyer Guide: Des Moines Area

Buying your first home in West Des Moines, Waukee, or nearby suburbs is exciting—and easier when you know the steps ahead of time.

Start with your budget (and pre-approval)

Before you fall in love with a listing, get clear on what you can comfortably afford each month, including taxes and insurance. A lender can issue a pre-approval letter so sellers know you are serious and qualified.

Choose a local agent early

A realtor who knows the Des Moines metro can help you compare neighborhoods, spot fair pricing, and write a competitive offer when inventory is tight.

Inspections and closing

After your offer is accepted, you will typically have a home inspection window. Use that time to understand the home’s condition. Before closing, you will review final numbers with your lender and title company, then get the keys.
